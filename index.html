The Problem

Decisions Disappear. Context Goes with Them.

Decisions are made across meetings, documents, and fragmented systems. What remains is the outcome. What disappears is the reasoning.

Lost Reasoning

Over time, organizations lose the context behind critical choices — the assumptions that shaped them, the alternatives that were weighed, and the constraints that narrowed the path.

Eroded Accountability

When decisions live in scattered documents and meeting notes, ownership becomes unclear. Who decided, on what basis, and who approved — these traces fade quickly.

Operational Drift

Without a record of original intent, execution diverges from the decision that authorized it. Teams inherit outcomes without understanding the rationale behind them.

Repeated Mistakes

Organizations revisit the same decisions because prior reasoning was never preserved. Institutional learning depends on institutional memory — which most enterprises lack.

The Platform

Decisions as Durable, Governed Objects

MagnaRix introduces a structured system where decisions become persistent enterprise records. Each decision captures the full context of how and why it was made.

Objectives & Outcomes

Define what the decision aims to achieve and what success looks like, anchoring all subsequent context to a clear intent.

Assumptions & Constraints

Surface the conditions taken as given and the boundaries within which the decision must operate — making implicit knowledge explicit.

Alternatives & Trade-offs

Capture what else was considered, why it was set aside, and what trade-offs were accepted — preserving the decision landscape.

Risks & Mitigations

Model probability, impact, and ownership of risks directly within the decision record, with mitigations linked to each.

Approvals & Ownership

Route decisions through governed approval workflows with clear accountability — who submitted, who reviewed, who approved.

AI Analysis & Summaries

Generate structured AI summaries, surface hidden assumptions, identify risk signals, and provide alternative perspectives tied to the specific decision.
