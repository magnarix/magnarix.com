The MagnaRix Platform

MagnaRix takes shape around a reality that most enterprise systems leave unaddressed: the decision itself — how it was formed, what shaped it, who authorized it — is an object that deserves structure, governance, and persistence.

The Decision Canvas

A structured environment for decision formation

The Decision Canvas is where each decision takes shape. It is a structured environment in which the components of a decision — its objectives, the constraints it operates within, the assumptions held at the time, the alternatives weighed, and the trade-offs accepted — are captured as distinct, structured elements.

This structure serves two purposes. In the moment, it disciplines the act of deciding — ensuring that relevant context is made explicit rather than implied. Over time, it creates a record that can be read, reviewed, and understood by anyone who encounters the decision later.

AI-Assisted Analysis

Advisory intelligence within the decision

MagnaRix includes AI-assisted analysis that operates within the context of each individual decision. It does not replace judgment. It works alongside it — surfacing assumptions that may need scrutiny, identifying patterns in the stated risks, generating structured summaries, and offering perspectives that the decision-maker may not have considered.

This analysis remains with the decision record. It is not ephemeral advice generated in a chat window. It becomes part of the decision's context, available to anyone who reviews or replays that decision in the future.

Risk & Mitigation

Risk as a dimension of the decision

In many organizations, risk assessment is a separate activity — conducted by different teams, stored in different systems, often disconnected from the decision that generated the risk in the first place.

In MagnaRix, risk exists as a first-class element of the decision record. Each risk carries a probability assessment, an impact evaluation, a mitigation strategy, and an assigned owner. These are not external annotations — they are part of what the decision is, and they persist with it.

Approval & Governance

Authority made traceable

A decision moves through stages — from formation to review to approval. At each stage, different people are involved, different questions are asked, and different accountabilities apply. MagnaRix governs this lifecycle with structured workflows where submission, review, and approval are explicit, timestamped, and permanent.

Once approved, a decision record is locked. The state at the moment of authorization is preserved. This is what makes governance meaningful: not the existence of an approval process, but the ability to see exactly what was approved, by whom, and on what basis.

Decision Memory

What remains after the people leave

Every decision in MagnaRix persists as an immutable record. This is institutional memory in its most durable form — not a knowledge base article written after the fact, but the actual record of the decision as it was formed and authorized.

When teams change, when leadership transitions, when the architect who chose the platform leaves — the reasoning persists. Future teams can read the decision as it was, not as someone later remembered it.

Replay & Audit

Temporal understanding

Decision replay allows any decision to be examined as it existed at a given point in time. What was known. What was assumed. What risks were accepted. Who had reviewed it, and whether approval had been granted.

This capability serves auditors, governance teams, and future decision-makers equally. It answers the question that retrospective reconstruction always struggles with: what did the organization actually know and believe when this decision was made?

Execution Alignment

Keeping action aligned with intent

MagnaRix carries the logic of the decision beyond the moment of approval, so that execution remains intelligible in relation to the choice that authorized it. The intent, constraints, and accountability of the original decision do not need to disappear once action begins.

When execution drifts from the decision that sanctioned it, work continues without reference to the reasoning that justified it. MagnaRix is designed to keep this connection visible, so that action can remain aligned with the decision from which it proceeds.

Architecture

Platform layers

MagnaRix is composed of six integrated layers, each preserving a distinct dimension of the decision lifecycle.

L6 Execution Alignment Layer Alignment of execution with original decision intent
L5 Advisory Intelligence Layer AI-assisted analysis, assumption surfacing, and pattern signals
L4 Decision Memory Layer Immutable decision records and institutional continuity
L3 Governance & Approval Layer Approval workflows, ownership, and audit trail
L2 Reasoning Layer Assumptions, alternatives, constraints, and risk formation
L1 Decision Canvas Structured decision formation and capture

Each layer of MagnaRix preserves a distinct dimension of the decision. Together, they form a system in which decisions are shaped with structure, examined with rigor, governed with accountability, and preserved with permanence.
